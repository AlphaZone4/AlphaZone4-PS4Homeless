The Problem

PlayStation Home players who have purchased a PlayStation 4 are in crisis.

Since PlayStation 4 does not support PlayStation Home, all these users are now Homeless.

To demonstrate the chaos of this situation, we've built this helpful chart to show the struggle existing PlayStation Home users face after buying a PlayStation 4.

As you can see, people already playing PlayStation Home have to either play something else, or face the almost impossible task of plugging their PlayStation 3 back in.
